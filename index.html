The Foreign Classics

This visualization presents foreign classics in a hierarchial manner, which could essentially be used as a book suggestion guide. The hierarchy is from the inner ring to the outer ring. The inner ring comprises of the different languages, all encoded in the global language code. The following are the language encodings:

  • de: Deutsch
  • fr: French
  • la: Latin
  • es: Spanish
  • pt: Portugese
  • tl: Tagalog
  • ru: Russian
  • nl: Dutch
